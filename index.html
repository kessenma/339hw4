Cookie Blog

Hey! Thanks for visiting! This website is dedicated to my first love-- cookies.

I have my opinions on what makes a good cookie and what makes a bad cookie, but so do my friends and family, so I sent an survey to a bunch of people asking for their opinions on cookies. I got back a bunch of responses, and I am going to showcase all of the responses on this website while learning accessible design practices.
